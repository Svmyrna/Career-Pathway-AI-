# Career-Pathway-AI-
Career PathwayAI empowers high school students to make confident academic and career decisions by translating real performance to personalized career pathways. It is layer in digital education platforms, it performs as a gap between learning, self-awareness, and future readiness, when it reduces misaligned academic choices and educational waste. 
# Career Pathway AI

> *Career clarity from real schoolwork — not surveys.*

**Live Prototype:** [careerpathwayai.lovable.app](https://careerpathwayai.lovable.app)

---

## What It Does

Career Pathway AI is an LMS-integrated AI platform that reads what students actually do in school — assignments, grades, feedback — and translates that into personalized career and education pathways. Updated every two weeks. No surveys. No extra work for teachers. Built directly into Canvas and Google Classroom.

---

## The Problem

High school students make life-defining decisions without real data about their own strengths. Counselors manage 400+ students each and can't track everyone. Meanwhile, every LMS in every school is already generating rich academic signals — and nobody is reading them.

---

## The Solution

Career Pathway AI connects to the LMS already in use, detects skill signals from real coursework, and delivers explainable pathway guidance to students, teachers, and parents — continuously, not once.

| Who | What they get |
|-----|--------------|
| Students | Strengths dashboard + career pathway with plain-language explanation |
| Teachers/Counselors | Skill signal summaries + growth trends, zero added workload |
| Parents/Districts | Bi-weekly alignment reports tied to measurable outcomes |

## North Star Metric — PAPR

**Pathway Alignment Progression Rate** = % of students whose course selections and performance patterns align with an evidence-based pathway, and where that alignment is stable over time.

Calculated bi-weekly from four signals: Course Enrollment Fit · Performance Fit · Skill Signal Fit · Stability Factor.

> PAPR measures whether guidance is actually changing decisions — not whether students opened a dashboard.

Healthy targets: **25–35%** (early stage) → **40–50%** (growth) → **55%+** (exceptional)

## Competition

Existing tools — Naviance, Xello, YouScience, Kuder — rely on one-time self-assessments. None continuously analyze real coursework. None update bi-weekly. None are LMS-native.

Career Pathway AI doesn't compete in the career assessment category. It creates a new one: **the academic intelligence layer** — continuous, performance-based, and built where students already are.
